## Voice Rules (Quick Reference)
- No exclamation marks
- No "we're excited" / "we're passionate" / "we love"
- No superlatives without structure ("best" → "most intentional")
- Italic serif for emotional words (home, life, belonging, field, enter)
- Short paragraphs. 2-3 sentences max.
- Let whitespace do the work
- The reader should feel assessed, not sold to
- Champagne gold (#C9B99A) for italic emphasis in headlines
